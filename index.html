This is the personal website of Nicki Vance.

I'm a user experience and product designer currently living in Melbourne, Australia.

I run a design consulting firm, Boxing Industries, where I help companies plan and design awesome, usable apps and web tools. I've worked with the United Nations, Adidas, Spotify, Comcast, Cigna, and AT&T, to name a few.

Moneyhawk is a personal finance app for Luddites that I designed and launched with Kevin Lynagh.

You'll usually find me traveling and working on various side projects in games, animation, and web development.

If you have any questions about how I created my projects, would like me to review your UX portfolio as a fellow professional, or if you'd like to hire me, email me at nicki@boxingindustries.com.
